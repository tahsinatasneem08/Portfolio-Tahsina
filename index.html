Featured Capstone

"Photovoltaic Implementation in Future Electric Vehicles"
+ Building a small scale Solar-Powered Electric Vehicle SPEV

This project explores the design and feasibility of a Solar-Powered Electric Vehicle (SPEV) for Bangladesh by integrating photovoltaic solar panels with electric vehicle technology. It analyzes energy generation, battery performance, vehicle power requirements, and regenerative braking to evaluate sustainable transportation solutions. The study proposes a solar-assisted EV system using lithium-ion batteries, BLDC motors, and MPPT-controlled solar charging to reduce dependence on fossil fuels.

The project highlights the environmental, economic, and technological benefits of adopting solar-powered transportation in regions with high solar potential such as Bangladesh.

A small-scale prototype was developed to demonstrate the practical implementation of a solar-powered electric vehicle, incorporating solar panels, a lithium-ion battery, a BLDC motor, and an Arduino-based control system. The prototype enabled real-time monitoring and control of power flow, validating the feasibility and performance of solar-assisted electric transportation.
